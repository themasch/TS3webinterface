Internet Explorer is not supported in any version.


Edit the 'config.inc.new.php' file, insert your TeamSpeak 3 Server IP and ServerQuery Port.
You can also change the language, at the moment 'en' or 'de'.
IMPORTANT: after that, you have to rename the 'config.inc.new.php' to 'config.inc.php'!
To finish the installation, copy the whole content of the archive including the modifications
on your server.

Have fun!


If there is a new release available, the software will show you a notification.



Please report bugs at the TeamSpeak forums
or send me an e-mail: robin@rk-programming.de
